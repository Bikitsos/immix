what is EDI?

Business-to-business Electronic Data Interchange or EDI is the is the concept of electronically communicating information, instead of on paper. Business documents between buyer and supplier in a supply chain or retail context. It consists of simple transferring purchase orders, invoices, payments, shipping notices and various other documents electronically in an industry standard formats and by its nature eliminating paper trails, improving operational efficiency.

How does EDI work

EDI replaces the manual processes involved in ordering and distribution, creating seamless electronic trading between both buyer and supplier in a supply chain.
EDI eliminates waiting times associated with manual processing, provides users with real-time product and stock level information, creates benchmarks for future sales forecasting and reduces overheads by creating an efficient business process.
