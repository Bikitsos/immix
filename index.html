b2b EDI services

data exchange (EDI)  and integration platform

what is EDI?
Business-to-business Electronic Data Interchange or EDI is the is the concept of electronically communicating information, instead of on paper. Business documents between buyer and supplier in a supply chain or retail context. It consists of simple transferring purchase orders, invoices, payments, shipping notices and various other documents  electronically in an industry standard formats and by its nature eliminating paper trails, improving operational efficiency. 
B2B collaboration can be done in a different levels depending of a customer infrastructure starting from: electronic invoices (web forms), batch-based electronic data interchange (EDI), to API enabled real-time integrations.

how does EDI work?
EDI replaces the manual processes involved in ordering and distribution, creating seamless electronic trading between both buyer and supplier in a supply chain.
EDI eliminates waiting times associated with manual processing, provides users with real-time product and stock level information, creates benchmarks for future sales forecasting and reduces overheads by creating an efficient business process.

POS system integration

keep up with changing technology trends

Point of Sale (POS) system integration is becoming increasingly important. The POS systems has moved from a traditional role in managing sales and purchases and a cash register function in scanning barcodes and printing receipts to swiping a credit/debit card and awarding loyalty points to a regular customer. 

As consumers nowadays choose to invest in experiences rather than just products, retailers need to respond to meet the needs of their customers. Customers don’t want to just walk into the shop, buy the product and leave because they could do the same in the comfort of their own home. But by creating a more immersive retail experience, retailers can drive people towards their stores and ensure they leave not just with your products but also memories.

The fusion of retail and entertainment “Retailtainment” is what will dominate the industry, an effort on the part of retailers to provide customers with fun, unique experiences that elevate shopping above anything it’s previously been: the best parts of boutique shopping with the speed and convenience of eCommerce.

Our role as POS system integrators is to enable POS application to successfully communicate with variety of new technology, add-on applications, marketing and consulting tools and a new hardware devices and gadgets .
