about us

A. what is immix?

immix (verb) "to mix together different elements, blend, combine, integrate, come together".

immix is a solution provider in the area of POS system integration, Technical support and an Electronic Data Interchange (EDI). 

B. what we do?

Our fields of expertise:
• POS systems integration (retail, POS, ERP)
• b2b electronic data interchange (EDI) 
• technical support services and maintenance

 C. why immix?

Having come from POS system implementations and IT support/maintenance background, through 30 years of collective experience, we have noticed that one of the biggest stumbling blocks to a smooth business system integration, or integration of any technology type, is connecting disparate systems. Trying to get different systems to talk to one another takes time and financial investment for both the customers and the suppliers involved.
